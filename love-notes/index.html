Love Notes

Singing Valentines

Looking for a fun, musical way to suprise a loved one during the week of Valentine's Day? Send a Medleys serenade!

We'll help you go all out for your boo, surprise your favorite professor, or make your friend's day—all while supporting the Downtown Women's Center. Last season, we delivered over 100 live and virtual Love Notes and raised almost $2,000!

Logistics for Love

  1. Pick a song that you would like Medleys to sing. We have over 10 fun options this year, including "Stand by Me," "Never Gonna Give You Up," and "You Belong With Me"!
  2. Let us know your preferred time for delivery. We'll be available to sing at various times throughout the week leading up to Valentine's Day!
  3. Choose a location! Available performance locations are limited to the UCLA campus and the immediate surrounding area (dorms, apartments, frat/sorority houses, etc.).
  4. Send us a payment in cash or through Venmo. All proceeds will be donated to the Downtown Women's Center.
  5. We will meet you at the specified time and location so that you can take us to the Love Note recipient. Happy Valentine's Day!
