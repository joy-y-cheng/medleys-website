Love Notes

Serenade Someone Special!

Are you looking for a fun, musical way to surprise a loved one this Valentine's Day? Well, look no further! Every year during the week of Valentine's Day, you can hire Medleys to serenade anyone you want.

We'll help you go all out for your boo, make your friend's day, surprise your favorite professor, or get your crush to notice you—all while supporting the Downtown Women's Center. Last season, we delivered over 100 live and virtual Love Notes and raised almost $2,000! (Seriously, our rizz is unmatched.)

Logistics for Love

  1. Pick a song that you would like Medleys to sing. We have over 10 fun options this year, including "Can't Take My Eyes Off You," "Never Gonna Give You Up," "Stand By Me," and "You Belong With Me"!
  2. Let us know your preferred time for delivery. We'll be available to sing at various times throughout the week leading up to Valentine's Day!
  3. Choose a location! Available performance locations are limited to the UCLA campus and the immediate surrounding area (dorms, apartments, frat/sorority houses, etc.). This could mean a street corner in front of their apartment, the front of their lecture hall, their dorm hallway as they're heading back from a shower—the possibilities are endless!
  4. Send us a payment in cash or through Venmo—we won't be able to perform until the payment has been received. All proceeds will be donated to the Downtown Women's Center.
  5. We will meet you at the specified time and location so that you can take us to the Love Note recipient. Happy Valentine's Day!
