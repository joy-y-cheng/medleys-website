Service

Love for People, Love for Music

Medleys is committed to spreading care, love, and joy through the healing power of music to those who need it most. Below are some of the ways in which we have served Los Angeles communities.

Partnerships and Events

Love Notes

Every year during the week of Valentine's Day, we raise money for the Downtown Women's Center through our Love Notes fundraiser during which we offer live and virtual serenades. This year, we delivered over 100 Love Notes and raised almost $2,000! You can support our cause by ordering a Love Note for a loved one.

Learn more about Love Notes

Learn more about the Downtown Women's Center

UCLA Health × UCLA People-Animal Connection

We have the honor of caroling for the amazing patients and dedicated staff at Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center and UCLA Santa Monica Medical Center alongside UCLA People-Animal Connection's talented dogs and wonderful team. We sing classic Christmas and Hanukkah carols for the December holidays and some of our Love Notes songs for Valentine's Day.

Learn more about UCLA People-Animal Connection

Read about us on KNX News

Reading to Kids

We enjoy reading to students at various elementary schools in Los Angeles with the passionate volunteers at Reading to Kids. For the March 2024 "Music and Entertainment"-themed reading club, we performed our setlist for the students at MacArthur Park Elementary School.

Learn more about Reading to Kids

Downtown Women's Center

We love singing for the incredible women at the Downtown Women's Center. In 2024, we celebrated the success of our annual Love Notes fundraiser by showcasing some of our love songs and also had the privilege of caroling during the holiday season. In the past, we've visited and performed at the Downtown Women's Center for Mother's Day.

Learn more about the Downtown Women's Center

Assistance League of Los Angeles Preschool Learning Center

In December 2024, we sang carols for the children at Assistance League of Los Angeles's Preschool Learning Center! We also had the joy of singing with the children and teaching them one of our favorite musical rounds.

Learn more about the Preschool Learning Center

Wise & Healthy Aging

We visited the Senior and Adult Day Center at Wise & Healthy Aging in December 2024 and sang carols to spread holiday cheer! We also shared a few songs from our fall setlist with the individuals there.

Learn more about Wise & Healthy Aging

UCLA Health × Chase Child Life Program

In 2024, we visited the pediatric unit at the UCLA Santa Monica Medical Center and had the honor of singing for the patients as part of the Chase Child Life Program. We sang a mix of pop songs and caroled during the holiday season to help ease the stress of a hospital stay.

Learn more about the Chase Child Life Program

Los Angeles Mission

Every winter, we host a clothing drive during our holiday concert and collect clothing donations for Los Angeles Mission.

Learn more about Los Angeles Mission

Los Angeles Unified School District

Every spring, we organize a school supplies drive during our final concert and donate school supplies to the Los Angeles Unified School District's Homeless Education Office.

Learn more about the Homeless Education Office

Habitat for Humanity × UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association

We competed in UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association's first-ever charity dodgeball tournament—the 2024 BelleBall Tournament—and won! As the winners, we had the opportunity to donate $1,000 to a charity of our choice. We chose Habitat for Humanity as the charity to receive the donation.

Learn more about Habitat for Humanity

Learn more about UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association

Assistance League of Los Angeles Family Day of Service

We joined Assistance League of Los Angeles in November 2024 for their Family Day of Service: "Sweet Dreams and Cozy Nights"! We made no-sew blankets for unhoused and foster youth in Los Angeles.

Learn more about Assistance League of Los Angeles

L.A. Cancer Challenge

In October 2024, we helped set up for the 27th Annual 5K Walk/Run which supports the Hirshberg Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research.
