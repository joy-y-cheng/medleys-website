Service

Love for People, Love for Music

Medleys is committed to spreading care, love, and joy through the healing power of music to those who need it most. Below are some of the ways in which we have served Los Angeles communities.

Current Partnerships and Recent Events

Downtown Women's Center

Every year during the week of Valentine's Day, we raise money for the Downtown Women's Center through our Love Notes fundraiser during which we offer live and virtual serenades. This year, we delivered over 100 Love Notes and raised almost $2,000! We had the privilege of celebrating the success of Love Notes by singing for the incredible women at the Downtown Women's Center.

Learn more about the Downtown Women's Center

Reading to Kids

For the March "Music and Entertainment"-themed reading club, we performed for the brilliant students at MacArthur Park Elementary School. We also expanded our volunteer experience beyond singing by reading to the students with the passionate volunteers at Reading to Kids.

Learn more about Reading to Kids

Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center with UCLA People-Animal Connection

This year, we had the honor of caroling for the amazing patients and dedicated staff at Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center alongside UCLA People-Animal Connection's talented dogs and wonderful team. We sang some classic Christmas and Hanukkah carols for the December holidays and some of our Love Notes songs for Valentine's Day.

Learn more about UCLA People-Animal Connection

Read about us on KNX News

Habitat for Humanity with UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association

We competed in UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association's first-ever charity dodgeball tournament—the BelleBall Tournament—and won! As the winners, we had the honor of donating $1,000 to a charity of our choice. We chose Habitat for Humanity as the charity to receive the donation.

Learn more about Habitat for Humanity

Learn more about UCLA Bruin Belles Service Association

Los Angeles Mission

Every year, we host a clothing drive during our holiday concert and collect clothing donations for Los Angeles Mission.

Learn more about Los Angeles Mission
