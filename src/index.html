Who We Are

Medleys A Cappella is a service-based group that combines a love for music and a desire to help the community.

Originally an organization of UCLA Medical School students, the undergraduate/graduate version was founded by Virginia Liu in 2003 and has become increasingly active since its conception.

What We Do

In our 21st year, we are dedicated to bringing the joy and healing of music to locations all around Los Angeles.

Some of our favorite places to perform have been St. John’s Hospital of Santa Monica, the Downtown Women’s Center, UCLA Dance Marathon, and the UCLA Farmers Market. Wherever we go, we hope to make a difference—even if that means brightening a single person's day!
